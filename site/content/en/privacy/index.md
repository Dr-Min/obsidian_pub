# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

This page is a baseline transparency notice for visitors to MINventory (`https://minventory.org/`). It is provided for general information and is not legal advice.

## Information that may be processed

MINventory is currently a static content-focused site. Even so, some limited information may still be processed, including:

- Basic technical information such as browser type, operating system, access time, and referring page
- Your email address and message if you contact the site directly
- Usage information collected through cookies or similar technologies if analytics or advertising features are enabled in the future

## Why information may be used

Information may be used for the following purposes:

- Running and maintaining the site
- Responding to inquiries
- Understanding which content is useful to readers
- Supporting future measurement for analytics, advertising, or recommendation systems

## Cookies and similar technologies

MINventory currently operates as a static site, so the practical use of cookies may be limited until additional services are enabled. However, cookies or similar technologies may be used in the future if analytics or advertising features are turned on.

For example, if advertising services are added, third-party vendors may use cookies to serve ads based on previous visits to this or other sites. If those features are activated, MINventory may also display consent or privacy choice tools where required.

## Advertising and third-party services

As of April 10, 2026, MINventory's ad setup may change over time. If services such as Google AdSense are enabled in the future:

- Third-party vendors may use cookies.
- Visitors in some regions may see consent or privacy choice messages.
- Relevant changes will be reflected on this page.

External links and embedded content may also be governed by the policies of those third-party services.

## Data retention

Direct inquiries and the minimum operational information needed to manage the site may be retained for a reasonable period. Unless a longer retention period is legally required, information that is no longer needed should be deleted or no longer used.

## External links

MINventory may include links to external sites. Those sites are governed by their own privacy practices and policies.

## Policy updates

This policy may be updated when site features, analytics, advertising, or contact methods change. If a material update is made, the last-updated date on this page should also be updated.

## Contact

Privacy-related questions can be sent to [contact@minventory.org](mailto:contact@minventory.org).
