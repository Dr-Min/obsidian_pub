# About

Last updated: April 10, 2026

MINventory is a bilingual publishing site about AI video prompts, image prompts, shot design, and visual direction language.

## What this site covers

- How to write AI video prompts
- Visual language such as shot composition, camera movement, lens choice, and lighting
- Prompt-term glossaries grounded in real references
- Archive-style guides that can be read in both Korean and English

## Editorial rules

- Core public pages are maintained in both Korean and English.
- Both versions use the same relative path and the same `translationKey` so the language switcher can pair them.
- Reference images should be real whenever possible, and the same displayed image should not be repeated without a clear reason.
- Topics that can change over time should be updated when needed.

## Who this site is for

- People who want to learn AI video prompting in a more structured way
- People building prompt libraries for video models such as Seedance
- People who want to use camera, lens, lighting, and motion language more precisely

## Monetization and recommendations

MINventory may include ads, affiliate links, recommendation links, or its own digital products in the future. If those features are activated, the relevant disclosures and policy pages will be updated.
