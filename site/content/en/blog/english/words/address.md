# address: why can it mean location, speak to, and deal with?

Q. Why can `address` mean a street location, talk to people, and handle a problem?

Many English words have several meanings.
If you look closely, those meanings often connect through one image.
`address` becomes clearer when you think of directing something toward a target.

## 1) Core Link at a Glance

- `address` = location
- `address` = speak to
- `address` = deal with

Shared feeling: `direct words, attention, or information toward a specific target`.

## 2) Step 1: Origin / Form

For learning, it is useful to see `address` as `ad + dress`.

`ad` = to, toward

`dress` = here, not clothing, but an older sense close to arrange or direct
