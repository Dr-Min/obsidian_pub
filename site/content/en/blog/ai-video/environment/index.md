# Environment and Space

Environment terms do more than name a place. They imply light, reflection, scale, texture, and social energy.

All visuals on this page are self-authored SVG reference images generated inside the repo.

Key idea: These terms help the model lock the shot into a recognizable physical context.

## urban street

A city street with traffic, signage, and public movement.

![[assets/ai-video/glossary/environment/urban-street.svg|1200]]

- Prompt fragment: `a lone subject moving through an urban street, medium shot, cinematic realism`
- What to notice: Think beyond the place name and consider depth, reflection, and scale.
