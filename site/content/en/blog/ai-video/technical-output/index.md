# Technical and Output Style

Technical output terms should not replace camera language, but they help steer the final finish and production style of the result.

All visuals on this page are self-authored SVG reference images generated inside the repo.

Key idea: These terms help define the finish level and production flavor of the final clip.

## 16:9

A wide aspect ratio that favors horizontal space and environmental framing.

![[assets/ai-video/glossary/technical-output/16-9.svg|1200]]

- Prompt fragment: `warehouse chase scene, 35mm lens, 16:9`
- What to notice: These are finishing labels that work best after camera language is already clear.
