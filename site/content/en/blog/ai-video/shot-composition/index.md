# Shot Composition

Composition terms are one of the fastest ways to change how a scene feels. A single switch from wide shot to close-up or from eye level to high angle can reshape the whole result.

All visuals on this page are self-authored SVG reference images generated inside the repo.

Key idea: These terms control subject scale, placement in frame, and the viewing angle of the shot.

## wide shot

A shot where the environment does more narrative work than the subject's detail.

![[assets/ai-video/glossary/shot-composition/wide-shot.svg|1200]]

- Prompt fragment: `a runner in a rainy street, wide shot, 50mm lens, soft neon reflections`
- What to notice: Check subject size and placement in the frame before layering style words.
