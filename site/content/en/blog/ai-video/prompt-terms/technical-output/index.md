# Technical and Output Style

This page has been rebuilt with **real photographic references**.  
Technical and output-style terms do not replace camera language. They sit later in the prompt and help define how the final result should feel as a finished piece.

## 16:9

16:9 is the default horizontal frame for general video delivery. It is the most natural format for wide spatial composition.

![16:9 reference](<https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/dd/Long_Island_City_New_York_May_2015_panorama_3.jpg/1280px-Long_Island_City_New_York_May_2015_panorama_3.jpg>)

- Prompt fragment: `16:9 aspect ratio, wide cinematic framing`
- Real reference: [Long Island City New York May 2015 panorama 3.jpg](<https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File%3ALong%20Island%20City%20New%20York%20May%202015%20panorama%203.jpg>)

## 4K

4K implies more than a number. In prompting, it usually signals that texture and fine detail should survive close inspection.

![4K reference](<https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/25/Weathered_wood_grain_texture_macro_closeup_detail.jpg/1280px-Weathered_wood_grain_texture_macro_closeup_detail.jpg>)

- Prompt fragment: `4K detail, crisp texture fidelity, high-resolution finish`
- Real reference: [Weathered wood grain texture macro closeup detail.jpg](<https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File%3AWeathered%20wood%20grain%20texture%20macro%20closeup%20detail.jpg>)

## live-action look

A live-action look asks the model to resolve light, skin, fabric, and texture more like photographed reality than animation.

![live-action look reference](<https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/46/La_Fouine_%28Laouni_Mouhid%29_-_Portrait_studio.jpg/1280px-La_Fouine_%28Laouni_Mouhid%29_-_Portrait_studio.jpg>)

- Prompt fragment: `live-action look, natural skin texture, photographic realism`
- Real reference: [La Fouine (Laouni Mouhid) - Portrait studio.jpg](<https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File%3ALa%20Fouine%20%28Laouni%20Mouhid%29%20-%20Portrait%20studio.jpg>)

## blockbuster style

Blockbuster style points toward large-scale spectacle, dense lighting, and a more epic production finish.

![blockbuster style reference](<https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/81/New_York_City_night_skyline_by_500px_1.jpg/1280px-New_York_City_night_skyline_by_500px_1.jpg>)

- Prompt fragment: `blockbuster style, epic city scale, high-impact night atmosphere`
- Real reference: [New York City night skyline by 500px 1.jpg](<https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File%3ANew%20York%20City%20night%20skyline%20by%20500px%201.jpg>)

## documentary style

Documentary style favors observation, candid timing, and less visibly staged presentation.

![documentary style reference](<https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/4c/Imagine_All_The_Children_%2877763091%29.jpeg/1280px-Imagine_All_The_Children_%2877763091%29.jpeg>)

- Prompt fragment: `documentary style, candid observation, unforced realism`
- Real reference: [Imagine All The Children (77763091).jpeg](<https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File%3AImagine%20All%20The%20Children%20%2877763091%29.jpeg>)

## commercial look

A commercial look aims for polished control, refined surfaces, and a premium product or brand finish.

![commercial look reference](<https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/14/System76_product_lemp11.webp/1280px-System76_product_lemp11.webp.png>)

- Prompt fragment: `commercial look, premium product finish, polished lighting control`
- Real reference: [System76 product lemp11.webp](<https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File%3ASystem76%20product%20lemp11.webp>)

## Summary

Technical and output-style language usually works best at the end of the prompt.

- frame format: `16:9`
- resolution/detail target: `4K`
- reality treatment: `live-action look`
- production finish: `blockbuster style`, `documentary style`, `commercial look`

These terms do not build the scene by themselves. They define **what kind of finished artifact the scene should resemble**.
