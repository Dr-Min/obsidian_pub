# Lighting

Lighting terms shape mood and dimensionality at the same time. The same frame can feel premium, eerie, harsh, or intimate depending on the light design.

All visuals on this page are self-authored SVG reference images generated inside the repo.

Key idea: These terms help define direction, softness, contrast, and whether visible practical sources are part of the frame.

## soft light

Light with soft shadow transitions and gentle skin rendering.

![[assets/ai-video/glossary/lighting/soft-light.svg|1200]]

- Prompt fragment: `studio interview setup, soft light, premium commercial texture`
- What to notice: Look at direction, softness, color, and what the light does to the subject edge.
